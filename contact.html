Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the raw data?

All data is publicly available in our GitHub repository. Water and sediment chemistry samples from 2016–2024 are documented with QA/QC protocols.

Are remediation technologies ready for deployment?

Phase 1 (monitoring) is immediately deployable. Phases 2–3 require pilot testing and community engagement. We support organizations interested in implementation.

How do I cite this research?

The peer-reviewed publication is available in the resources section. Please reference the full citation and link to our GitHub for reproducible analysis.

Can universities collaborate on extensions?

Yes! We welcome academic partnerships for air quality monitoring, health impact assessments, and ecosystem recovery studies. Contact us directly.
