River Remedy
Website UI Kit

The visual language that powers the Pilcomayo basin contamination analysis: Bolivia-flag accents, flowing water motifs, editorial typography, and pastel-tinted research surfaces. Every token, component, and pattern documented here is wired into River Remedy.html.

01 — Brand

Logo & Wordmark

The River Remedy mark pairs a concentric ripple — water expanding from a single source — with the Playfair Display wordmark. The "Remedy" half is set in water-blue to anchor the flowing-water theme. Always reserve clear space equal to the ripple diameter on all sides.

02 — Colors

Color System

A Bolivia-anchored palette: red, yellow, green, and orange as primary identity colors, supported by deep forest neutrals, water blues, and pastel-tinted pastel surfaces. Bolivia colors carry semantic weight — red for alerts and risk, yellow for findings and partial states, green for outcomes and success, orange for legacy/historical context.

03 — Typography

Type System

Two typefaces carry the entire system. Playfair Display handles all headings — its Times-adjacent silhouettes give the work academic gravity, and italic serves as a soft accent for poetic phrases ("Pilcomayo River"). Inter handles every other surface for clarity at small sizes and high screen density.

07 — Components

Callouts

Four semantic callout treatments — alert, finding, success, info — with a left-border accent and pastel fill. Used inline in narrative sections to surface key context without breaking flow.

⚠
Critical — WHO Threshold Exceeded Lead concentrations at Hotspot A and C exceed WHO drinking water guidelines by 4.2× during peak wet-season flow events.
◈
Finding — Sediment Partitioning Mean K_d coefficient of 0.82 indicates strong sediment-binding for Pb, increasing downstream transport during high-flow periods.
✓
Outcome — Targeted Remediation Feasible Geochemical modeling identifies three high-priority intervention zones with projected 40–65% metal load reduction.
ⓘ
Methodology Note Spatiotemporal kriging interpolation applied across 20 monitoring stations · pilcomayo.net 2008–2024 dataset.

09 — Patterns

Wave Dividers

Subtle SVG waves separate every content section — the literal manifestation of the flowing-water aesthetic. Always render between two solid section colors so the wave sits cleanly. Three rhythm variants: gentle, layered, and offset.
